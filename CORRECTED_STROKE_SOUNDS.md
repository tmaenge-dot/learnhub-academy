### Why Sound-Based Names?

In Pitman Shorthand, strokes represent **SOUNDS**, not letters:

- **ITH** = the sound you hear in "**th**in", "ba**th**"

- **ISH** = the sound you hear in "**sh**e", "fi**sh**"
- **ZHEE** = the sound you hear in "mea**s**ure", "vi**s**ion"
